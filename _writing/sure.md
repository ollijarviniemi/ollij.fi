# Sure about that?

*Anna:* Okay, I've put 100 balls in this urn. Some of them are red. I won't give you any other info. How many red balls do you guess are in there?

*Bella:* 50

*Camilla:* 20

*Anna:* Good, now I'll draw a ball out... Oh, it's red! Let's set it aside. Okay, what's your guess now?

*Bella:* 49

*Camilla:* 40

*Bella:* What, a red ball was just removed, but now you're claiming the number of red balls is higher than before?! Are you absolutely sure about that?

*Camilla:* No.
